About Me

I am a high school student from San Jose, CA

My Story

Born and raised into an immigrant family, I became resourceful because of my family's low income. I taught myself how to program out of necessity when my parents had to move their acupuncture clinic.

The success I found while creating my parents' acupuncture website inspired me to pursue other computer science projects. Even though now I mostly focus on creating ios apps, websites, and machine learning algorithms, one thing hasn't changed, I am self-made.

If you would like to see some of my work, please check out my projects page, where I document both my previous and ongoing projects.
